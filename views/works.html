Pacman

Pacman is a very hard worker character. It will do everything to eat as many coins as possible. When he is very exited he can even eat fruits and ghost for the 19 first levels

The ghosts

The four ghosts have a personnality that will help them eat Pacman will teaming up.

Blinky

Blinky will chase Pacman. Be mindful of U-turns.

Pinky

Pinky try to anticipate Pacman movements to end in front of him.

Inky

Inky will try to face Pacman as well with a more elaborate way of thinking. He takes into consideration Blinky moves accordingly. Together they try to surround Pacman.

Clyde

Clyde is a bit different from the other ghosts. He feel the courage to follow Pacamn when he is far away (as Blinky is). But as soon as he came too close he is running away to his corner.
